Primary Intake Flow
Screen 1

Intake — Normal State

Nurse opens intake for a new patient. AI parses chief complaint in real-time and suggests structured fields in sidebar. No critical flags detected.

Triage Nurse AI Active
Screen 2

Intake — Moderate Flag Alert

AI detects a critical flag with 70–89% confidence. Alert card appears in sidebar with flag name, confidence score, and source text citation. Nurse must acknowledge.

Moderate Alert Triage Nurse
Screen 3

Intake — Critical Flag Alert

AI confidence ≥ 90%. Urgent alert banner activates, alert card pulses in red. Audio alert indicator shown. Nurse must explicitly confirm before proceeding.

Critical Alert Triage Nurse
Screen 4

Intake Summary — Nurse Sign-off

All AI-suggested and nurse-entered fields shown for final review before EMR submission. Each field tagged by source. Nurse signs off and submits. Nothing enters EMR without this step.

Pre-submission Triage Nurse
Supporting Screens
Screen 5

Queue Priority Panel

Read-only acuity distribution dashboard. Shows current waiting room patient count by ESI level, wait times, and chief complaint summaries. Updated as intakes complete.

Read Only All Roles
Screen 6

Manual Mode — EMR Offline

EMR integration failure triggers silent fallback to manual mode. AI sidebar is disabled. Full manual intake form remains operational. No data loss. Purple banner signals degraded state.

Degraded State Fallback
