# AI Product Leadership Roadmap

---

# Objective

Develop the ability to:

- Lead AI products
- Communicate AI system tradeoffs
- Evaluate AI workflows
- Design enterprise AI solutions
- Work effectively with engineering teams
- Build AI product strategy
- Handle AI reliability and operational concerns

---

# Core Product Leadership Areas

| Area | Focus |
|---|---|
| AI Product Thinking | When and why to use AI |
| AI Evaluation | Measuring quality and reliability |
| AI Architecture Communication | Explaining systems clearly |
| AI Workflow Design | Designing operational AI systems |
| AI Business Strategy | ROI, adoption, prioritization |
| AI Governance | Risk, compliance, human review |
